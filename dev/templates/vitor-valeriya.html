Vitor & Valeriya

Vitor Mendes (Cape Verde/Portugal)
Kizomba Prague founder, instructor, multi-talented dancer, choreographer and entertainer with international recognition. His dance styles are Kuduro-Decale, Kizomba, Afro-Contemporary, Capoeira, Abstract dance, Street dance, Hip Hop, Afro-Street Dance.

He also has the ability to fuse different dance styles and combine them into one as he call it - Dance Fusion. Example of his success is Patti Boulaye's Sun Dance, dance musical in London or participation at Český Lev film awards.

Vitor has joined a Bollywood Dance performance - Shiva's Wheel of Life at ABC Theatre - Live Musical and Prague Adaptation of Arthur Schnitzler's "La Ronde" at Malé Vinohradské Theatre. You could read big interviews with him in newspapers MF Dnes and also in magazine Lidé a Země. Currently he promotes Luso-Afro-Latino culture and organizes Multicultural Music & Dance Festival Afro-Bailar in Prague, Czech Republic. You can also join his other workshops and classes of Abstract Dance and Afro-Fitness. Vitor also offers private classes and service of personal trainer. Vitor has official Fitness Instructor Certificate.

Valeriya Romanova (Ukraine)
Valeriya is from Ukraine but she lives in Prague for several years. She is a talented dancer and has experience with different types of dance such as salsa, bachata, zouk, reggaeton, tango, merengue, but her biggest passion became Kizomba.

Valeriya took six months intense training with Vitor, in which they went through not only the dance steps and variations, but primarily musicality, body expression and styling.
