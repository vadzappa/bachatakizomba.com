Mister Tecas & Miss Jo

Mister Tecas & Miss Jo… the duo that has introduced Semba in France! Based in Paris, Mister Tecas is the founder of the first school of traditional Angolan dances in France, called "kizomba.fr". It is known for having the first set of regular courses of kizomba in Paris!

In addition, Mister Tecas has been the instructor of many professors of Kizomba and Semba in France, as well as judging in many national and international competitions of Kizomba and Semba. In 2012, he was called to Angola: "the cradle of the Kizomba and the Semba" to choreograph at "Bounce Angola", the largest dance competition of the country.
