DJ The Joker is Wild

DJing salsa in Riga from 2006. Producing salsa events in Riga with international dancers and Djs. Djing at Riga Salsa Festival, Tallinn Latin Festival, Berlin Salsa Congress and regular Estonian and Lithuanian events.

He took his first salsa lesson in September of 2006. In a year he joined the SalsaLovers showgroup. During the next year his interest in salsa music turned into his first job as salsa Dj in different salsa events in Riga. He has grown and developed since then and has become the best salsa Dj in Latvia. He is not only dancing, visiting different festivals, performing and djing, but also gathering groups to different festivals and organizing various salsa parties in Riga.
