Kaem & Marine

Kaem & Marine are the founders of the Fuz'Style Kizomba, a very human and artistic approach of kizomba that strives to create an intense fusion between the two partners during the dance. To reach such a depth in kizomba, they invented new technics of styling, body and emotional connection. The outcome is a stunning kizomba experience with a huge creativity in moves.

Their modern tango, hip hop, ballet, contemporary dance and buddhist zen influences, makes their style unique and their workshops very technical and highly emotional.
