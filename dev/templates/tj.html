DJ Tj

Born in 1980 in Guadeloupe, it's in the 90s, period of the vinyl turntable, that DJ Tj, then 14-year-old, discovers himself a real passion for the music and decide become DJ.

His style, his personality and his wide knowledge of musical genres, earned then the teenager to get noticed and formed by the group "Cosmique One" (Radio, Technique of Mix and DJs). Since then, he didn't stopped enriching its mobile disco during any events he participates.

The DJ who works hard, developed his technique of mix as well as his musical directory so that lot of clubs notice him and recruit him.

In 2009, Tj makes even a tour of 2 years in the French clubs like official DJ "Havana Club". With an capacity of adaptation certain and going always further in improving his art, it's quite naturally that Tj knew how to assimilate and integrate the Lusophone musicality into its directory. Today, DJ Tj is an accomplished DJ, who's able to making you travel from a musical universe to the other one whatever is your event.
