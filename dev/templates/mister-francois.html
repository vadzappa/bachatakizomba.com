DJ Mister Francois (UK)

I was introduced to Kizomba around 4 years ago and was immediately captivated by the sound and rhythm of the music and the moves. This developed into a real passion and thirst to learn the dance in order to interpret the music using my feet, mind, body and soul. With my several years of experience DJ-ing in Soul, Reggae and RnB clubs across the UK, this eventually led to me transition as a DJ in the kizomba scene. To day I have played at the following locations: Kizz Afro - Moscow BKC - Budapest 1st Kizomba festival - Bucharest River Boat Kizomba festival - Bratislava KizMi - Milan Na Passada Monthly Party - Holland As well as regular nights at different venues in London.

My mission is simple: to keep the crowd hypnotized by the music, moving to the beat and always leave them wanting more.
