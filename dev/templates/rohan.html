Rohan Brown

Very well known and established instructor from Birmingham, England. His pre salsa career took him around the world in careers such as: pro basket ball, semi pro athletics, cat walk model, university graduate and international development officer with the United nations. Rohans first encounter with salsa was whilst stationed in Puerto Rico. Since then has pursued his career to the highest level and taught, DJ, compared and promoted him self around the world. He could be easily described as one of the hardest working men in salsa and one of the best all rounders on the international Scene.

His jazz background helps him to focus his teaching on the basic principles of fluid body movement and personal development and having lots of fun. He is certainly the total package.
