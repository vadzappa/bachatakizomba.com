Riccardo Recenati (Italy)

Originally from Italy and actually living in Poland, is the leader of the international team "Universo Salsero Dance Company" that he founded in 2006. Musician and sportman since the childhood he began his dance formation at the time when he was studying at University, learning all main salsa styles, bachata and other caribbean dances and extending later his interest also to completely different fields like street dances and jazz.

In 2004 he started to teach salsa (L.A., New York, Puerto Rico and Cuban style), bachata and other related dances in Italy and in Poland and he already had some experience teaching and performing on international stages since 2005. In the period from 2005 and 2009 he participated and won several competions and he finally moved to Wroclaw, in Poland, where he opened his dance school "Fly Dance Academy" and where he organizes some international dance events.

First official bachata teacher in Poland (he started to teach this style in this country already in 2004) he is a popular multi-style artist and instructor in salsa and bachata festivals around Europe. His hot italian charisma, amazing technique, creativity and passion for almost all kinds of dances make his choreographies original and very different each other.
