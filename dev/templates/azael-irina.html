Azael & Irina

Azael is an energetic and charismatic dancer, who can combine in a dance speed and high energy with romance and sensuality. Thanks to his unique style he has become very popular around Europe in the last year, especially as a Bachata and Kizomba instructor. He begun his career in Monterrey Mexico where he won the Mamboss Salsa Open competition twice and trained under Salzumba, one of the most important dance companies in Mexico, former school of other great artists like Adolfo Indacochea. In 2012 he decided to continue with his project in Europe and he moved to Dublin Ireland where he was named the Irish Bachata Champion, he created his own company AfroLatin Fever.

Irina dances for many years now and she is an instructor at SalsaLovers Latvia school. Gracious and elegant she can adopt to any music, any mood, any partner. A person who can be called "energy drink" or "perpetual engine" as in life as on the dance floor. Irina was a member of SalsaLovers showgroup, which is representing Latvia in International Salsa Scene in Germany, Poland, Estonia, Lithuania etc. The main thing they are trying to make in workshops is to teach people to dance in harmony with music and with partner. They still believe that the dance needs to be comfort for both in couple. The most important value is theirs capacity to create via students groups a friendly community and a positive environment, encouraging them to perform and express themselves through Afro Latin Dance.
