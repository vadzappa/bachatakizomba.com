George Daniels

George Daniels who is known by his passionate, energetic and playful dance style comes from Cape Town, South Africa and has been dancing since the age of 15. He started teaching dancing bachata, LA salsa and merengue ten years ago and now living in Norway he is spreading his passion to dance not only to Norwegian people but also internationally due to his trips to different dance festivals and congresses worldwide.
