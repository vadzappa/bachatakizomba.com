DJ Babacar

Originally from Senegal, DJ Babacar came to Europe as teenager and developped his love for music and dancing (HipHop, Jazz, African, French Caribbean and Latin). This lead him to play piano and the trompette. When his work took him to Porto he found his passion for DJing, settled there and took a course in Djing and Music Production. He has become since resident DJ of the famous El Sonero, Bar Tradicion and now Muevete Bar. He supported Buraka som Systema, Makongo, Mika Mendes, Neuza, To Semedo, Eddu, Gama, Garimpeiros etc… during their concert in Porto and has played in Lisbon (Pacha, Via Rapida, Salsa 114 and a few more) as well as Barcelona and Madrid, London France (Paris, Lille). His knowledge in Salsa, African Rythms and more urban tunes, and his love for dancing (he is a great salsero and teach alongside his wife) makes him a DJ that keeps you happily dancing all night… Love the life … like the life and enjoy the life, always with music, Music is my blood. Keep in peace.
