Morenasso & Anais

Angolan by nationality, professor, dancer and choreographer of Kizomba, Semba and Kuduro. Born in Luanda, the capital of Angola, and well-experienced in Angolan traditional and carnival dances from the very childhood.

Reputed and worldwide renowned professor. Lecturer in schools Salsabor in Paris, Salsabor Bobigny, Alatica in Bordeaux and Planete Monde in Marseille. Art director of Sensualonda Compagy. Acts as a professor in different cities of France (Paris, Laon, Reims, Rouen, Bordeaux, Toulouse, Montpellier, Nice, Cannes, Marseille, Nantes, Rennes, Tours, Lille, etc…)

Choreographer of the first Kizomba studio in France (Salsabor school). Dancer for the 30-year anniversary of Kassav at Stade de France.

Official representative of France at Africa Dançar international competition of kizomba dance in Portugal, 2010.

Participated as dancer and lecturer in numerous festivals of kizomba worldwide (Swimming Kizomba Paris, Festival de Rue Kizomba Love Bruxelles, Bordo Mambo Festival et Rueda Festival Bordeaux, Festival Tempo Latino Vic, Festival Salsa Del Mar Sables d'olonne, Like Festival Lisbonne, Criola Festival Alicante, Festival Sao Pedro do Moel Porto, Festival Danses Du Monde Dakar, Tabarka Festival Tunisie etc…).

Morenasso met Anaïs in November 2009, and they created SENSUALONDA. Creating Sensualonda is not a coincidence. Sensualonda "Sensual wave" stems from the way they feel and live this dance, the physical connection and vibration emanating from their union.
