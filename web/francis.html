Francis Andriana

There is no need to introduce Francis to Latvian salsa community. Most of you know him as a fantastic teacher with endless source of humor, energy and inspiration. He is a director of ChanGo' Salsa festival, and also created shows and performed for casinos in Las Vegas, Barcelona, Tunis, Ljubljana and Sydney. Being a well-known instructor and performer, he nevertheless travels regularly abroad for his trainings, particularly in New York and Cuba to enrich and develop his salsa style. In Riga, you will have an opportunity to learn his elegant, dynamic and sensual style.
